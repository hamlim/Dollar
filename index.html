FAQ:

Some (In)Frequently Asked Questions.

  • What is :dollar: (dollar)?
    • :dollar: (dollar) is a simple expense manager, allowing you to track your expenditures and offering some analytics on your expenses.
  • Should I be concerned about using this service?
    • Not at all, :dollar: (dollar) doesn't have any access to your bank accounts, nor does it store any private information regarding your purchases. Only price, category, location, and notes are stored.
  • Why should I use this service over things like spreadsheets or Mint?
    • :dollar: (dollar) is quickly accessible from any device that has an internet connection. Unlike other alternatives, :dollar: (dollar) is barebones and simple to use for anyone!
  • Why did you make this?
      • I used to track my expenses on a Google Sheet, however I realized I was wasting time on adding items to this sheet (especially on mobile) so I decided to make an alternative that is easy to use!
